1. Introduction

Welcome to OneNine's website. This privacy policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our website and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Data Collection and Use

We use Google Analytics to understand the traffic and types of visitors to our website. Therefore, Google Analytics collects data about your visit to our website. This may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our website that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data. For more information on how Google processes your data with Google Analytics, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Safeguarding your data pages.

3. Use of Data

The data collected is used to improve your experience on our website by understanding how our visitors interact with our site. For more information on use of data, please refer to section #2 above.

4. Data Protection

We value your privacy and take the protection of your data seriously. Your personal data is never sent directly to our computers or servers. All cookie data is managed directly by Google via Google Analytics. For more information on data protection, please refer to section #2 above.

5. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This data never touches our computers or servers. For more information on disclosure of data, please refer to section #2 above.

6. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
