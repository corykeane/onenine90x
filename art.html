Rules

Any real-life hand-crafted art is accepted. This includes drawings with pencil, pen, markers, paint, crayons, etc. You can also make a sculpture, diorama, or any other real-world craft project.

NO. Due to excessive cheating, we are not allowing any digital art. There were a couple legitimate digital art submissions before this prohibition became live. Those few will count, and the artists already know who they are. PHYSICAL MEDIA ONLY.

TRACING IS NOT ALLOWED. You may not trace a pre-existing image that you did not hand-draw yourself.

You must make sure to both ♻️ Retweet and ❤️ Like the original contest tweet.

In order to submit your artwork to the contest, reply directly to the original contest tweet with a photo of your artwork.

Make sure to write your @ Twitter username on your artwork! It must be written on using the same drawing method that you used to create the artwork (e.g. markers if you used markers to draw).

You're only required to submit 1 piece of artwork. However, if you want to submit more, make sure you only submit 3 submissions maximum.

Your artwork must be in some way related to Fortnite, and have at least one element of a Winter theme (e.g. snow, ice, santa hat, winter holiday, etc.).

The last day for submissions is December 31st.

FAQ

Upload a photo of your artwork as a reply on the original contest tweet. Make sure to read all the rules too.

The final submission date is December 31st, 2023. The judges will have about 2 weeks to cast their votes and choose the winners. Winners will be announced, and prizes will be awarded on January 12th, 2024.

NO. Due to excessive cheating, we are not allowing any digital art. There were a couple legitimate digital art submissions before this prohibition became live. Those few will count, and the artists already know who they are. PHYSICAL MEDIA ONLY.

There are 5 judges, each getting 5 grand prize votes. The judges will remain anonymous, but keep in mind they are all themselves artists too. Art will be judged on the skill, and creativity. If there is a tie on any submissions, a coin will be flipped to determine which takes the larger prize. The grand prizes will be chosen based on the most votes received.

3 of the 5 judges each get 1 "Honorable Mention" vote to choose the honorable mentions winners.

Finally, the Random Winners will be chosen by a randomize wheel spinner.
